About Dr. Xinde Zhang

Dr. Xinde Zhang is an Associate Teaching Professor at the Sam M. Walton College of Business, University of Arkansas. He specializes in financial data analytics, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. Dr. Zhang is dedicated to integrating cutting-edge technology into finance education, helping students develop practical skills that are essential in today's data-driven world.

Teaching Philosophy

Dr. Zhang's teaching approach emphasizes the integration of technology in the classroom. He uses tools such as Python programming, Dataiku, and generative AI (e.g., ChatGPT) to enhance the learning experience. His goal is to foster a problem-solving mindset in students, preparing them for successful careers in finance and data analytics.

Courses Developed

  • Advanced Financial Modeling
  • Financial Data Analytics I & II
  • Graduate courses in the Master of Finance program

Achievements

  • Financial Management Association International 2022 Innovation in Teaching Award, Runner-up
  • Published multiple peer-reviewed articles in high-quality journals
  • Developed and launched the Financial Analytics concentration for Finance majors

Contributions

Dr. Zhang has significantly contributed to the development of the Financial Data Analytics concentration and the Master of Finance program at the University of Arkansas. He is also involved in various service activities, including mentoring junior faculty and participating in departmental committees.
